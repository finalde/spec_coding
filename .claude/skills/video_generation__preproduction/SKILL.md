# Video Generation Preproduction

This skill merges structural planning and continuity locking into one preproduction step.

## Goal

Before prompt writing begins, produce:

- episode or scene blueprint
- Visual Bible
- Continuity Bible
- reusable locked wording blocks

## Workflow

### 1. Define the audience promise

State:

- who the video is for
- what feeling or payoff it delivers
- why the viewer keeps watching

### 2. Break into scene units

Convert the requested runtime into 15-second scene units by default.

For each scene, define:

- label
- purpose
- dominant emotion
- key action
- what must stay fixed

### 3. Lock the visual layer

Produce copy-verbatim blocks for:

- style
- character identity
- wardrobe / props
- environment
- lighting
- camera language

### 4. Write the Continuity Bible

Separate:

- must stay fixed
- allowed variation
- evolution across scenes
- drift recovery lines

## Output Format

```markdown
# Preproduction Pack

## Executive Summary
- ...

## Scene Unit Plan
| Scene | Purpose | Emotion | Key Action | Continuity Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

# Visual Bible
...

# Continuity Bible
...

## Reusable Locked Blocks
- Character: ...
- Environment: ...
- Camera: ...
```

## Rule

Do not move to storyboarding until the locked wording is strong enough to be reused verbatim.
