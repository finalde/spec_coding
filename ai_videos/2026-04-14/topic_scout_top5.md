## 1. Paper Phone | 纸手机

### Overview

**Mode**: Short
**Duration**: 40s
**Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
**Characters**: 1 (sole character — 6-year-old child)
**Source/Trend**: The AI short film "纸手机" went viral across Douyin and Bilibili in early 2026, proving that child-centric emotional stories with simple props are the highest-engagement format. Single-character child stories with parent-absence themes consistently trigger mass sharing ("tag your parents" comments). AI-generated "talking objects" and imaginative-play formats are trending on TikTok.
**One-line pitch**: A child draws a paper phone and "calls" their always-busy parent — and the paper phone rings back.
**Hashtags**: #AI视频 #纸手机 #童心 #亲情 #AIVideo #PaperPhone #情感短片 #SeedanceAI #治愈系 #催泪 #童年 #想念

**Drift Risks**: Child faces are hard for AI — proportions (big head, small features) can drift. Crayon drawings on paper must stay consistent. The "paper phone" prop must look the same across shots.
**Mitigation Strategies**: Lock child face to "round face, large dark eyes, small nose, chubby cheeks" in every prompt. Specify paper phone as "白色A4纸对折，蜡笔画的绿色长方形屏幕，红色圆形按钮" consistently. Keep the child seated to minimize body proportion drift.

### Narrative

A child sits alone at a small wooden desk in a dimly-lit apartment. The room is sparse — a single desk lamp casts a warm yellow circle on the desk surface. Outside the window, the city skyline is blue-grey dusk. The child is drawing with crayons on a piece of white paper, tongue poking out in concentration. We see what they're drawing: a rectangle with a green screen and a red circle button — a phone.

The child carefully tears the paper along the edges, folds it in half, and holds it up — a paper phone. They lift it to their ear, close their eyes, and whisper. For a moment nothing happens. The room is silent except for the hum of a distant refrigerator.

Then the desk lamp flickers. The warm light brightens, spreading beyond its circle to fill the room with golden warmth. The child opens their eyes wide — on the paper phone's "screen," the green crayon rectangle is now softly glowing. The child's face transforms from loneliness to pure, radiant joy — a smile so wide it crinkles their eyes shut. They press the paper phone tighter to their ear, nodding as if hearing a voice, whispering back.

The camera slowly pulls back. We see the full room — the child alone at the desk, bathed in impossible golden light, talking into a folded piece of paper. On the desk beside them, a real phone sits face-down, its screen dark. The golden light fades to the original desk lamp warmth. The child is still smiling, still whispering. Fade to black.

### Visual Style

- **Overall Style**: Cinematic photorealistic, warm vs cold contrast, intimate documentary feel
- **Reference Look**: A24 film aesthetic — shallow DoF, warm practicals, naturalistic lighting
- **Color Strategy**: Cold grey-blue rain (Scenes 1–2) → warm amber interior (Scene 3). The doorway is the transition point.
- **Style Anchors**: "4K电影级画质，冷灰蓝雨天对比暖黄室内，浅景深，猫毛皮质感真实，老人皮肤纹理细腻，A24纪实风格"

### Music / Audio Direction

- **Overall Mood**: Solo piano, extremely slow (40 BPM), minor key. Think Nils Frahm.
- **Arc**: Rain ambience only (Scene 1) → single piano note when eyes meet → piano melody when door opens (Scene 2) → full warm piano when cat is on lap (Scene 3) → fade to silence
- **Key Moments**: First eye contact = first piano note. Door crack of light = melody begins. Cat on lap = musical resolution in major key.

### Why This Works for AI Video

- **Virality angle**: "A stray cat chose him" triggers instant emotional response. Cat content is the #1 category on both Douyin and TikTok by volume. Combined with the "silver-haired emotional" format (7B+ plays on Douyin AI charts), this is a double-viral formula.
- **AI generation strengths**: Shot/reverse-shot avoids rendering human+cat together (the hardest case). Each subject gets dedicated close-ups. Rain, warm doorway light, and indoor lamp scenes are all proven AI-friendly. The cat is mostly still (curled up, sitting).
- **Rewatch / engagement hooks**: "I'm adopting a cat RIGHT NOW" comments. The door-crack-of-light image is iconic and shareable. Cat lovers + lonely elderly empathy = comment flood.
