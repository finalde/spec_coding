# 一条路的蜕变 | The Road That Changed Everything

## Title
**中文**: 一条路的蜕变
**English**: The Road That Changed Everything

## Description
**中文**: 同一个视角，五次快门，记录一个中国农村小镇从泥泞破败到焕然一新的全过程。路还是那条路，家还是那个家，但一切都不一样了。
**English**: Same angle, five frames — witness a Chinese rural village transform from muddy decay to vibrant renewal. The road remains, the homes remain, but everything has changed.
