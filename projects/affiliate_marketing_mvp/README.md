## 1. Overview & How the Money Flows

```
You pay Google    →  Google shows your ad to people searching "vpn deal"
Someone clicks   →  They land on YOUR page (not NordVPN directly)
They click your  →  They go to NordVPN via your tracked affiliate link
  CTA button
They buy          →  NordVPN pays you $100+ commission
```

**Why this works**: NordVPN pays you $100+ for each customer. A Google click
costs $0.50–$1.50. If even 1 in 50 clickers buys, you profit. The math is
heavily in your favor because the commission is high relative to click cost.

**Why a landing page in the middle**: Google Ads (and most ad platforms) will
ban you if you send traffic directly to an affiliate link. The landing page
also "warms up" the visitor so they're more likely to buy.

## 3. Sign Up for the Affiliate Program

### 3.1 Go to the affiliate page

- Open your browser
- Navigate to: `https://nordvpn.com/affiliates/`
- Click **"Join Now"** or **"Become a Partner"**

### 3.2 Fill in the application

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Name | Your real name |
| Email | Your real email (you'll need to verify it) |
| Website/URL | If you don't have one yet, enter `https://yourname.carrd.co` (you'll create this in the next step) |
| Traffic source | Select "Search/PPC" or "Content/Blog" |
| Monthly visitors | Be honest — "0-1000" is fine for a new account |
| How will you promote | Write: "Google Search Ads driving traffic to a VPN comparison/review landing page" |

### 3.3 Wait for approval

- Check your email for a confirmation link — click it
- Approval usually takes **1-48 hours** (often instant)
- If rejected: try Surfshark's affiliate program instead (same process, similar payouts)

### 3.4 Get your affiliate link

Once approved:

1. Log into the affiliate dashboard
2. Look for **"Affiliate Links"** or **"Tracking Links"** section
3. Copy your unique link — it will look something like:
   `https://go.nordvpn.net/aff_c?offer_id=15&aff_id=XXXXX`
4. **Save this link** — you'll paste it into your landing page button

### 3.5 Verify your link works

1. Open an incognito/private browser window
2. Paste your affiliate link into the address bar
3. Confirm it redirects to the NordVPN pricing page
4. Check your affiliate dashboard — you should see 1 click registered

## 5. Set Up Google Ads

### 5.1 Create a Google Ads account

1. Go to `https://ads.google.com`
2. Click **"Start Now"**
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Google will try to push you into a **"Smart Campaign"** — **DO NOT** use this

### 5.2 Switch to Expert Mode

This is critical. Smart campaigns give you no control.

1. Look for **"Switch to Expert Mode"** at the bottom of the setup wizard
2. Click it
3. You'll see the full campaign creation interface

### 5.3 Create a Search campaign

1. Click **"+ New Campaign"**
2. Campaign objective: Select **"Website traffic"**
3. Campaign type: Select **"Search"**
4. Enter your landing page URL when prompted

### 5.4 Campaign settings

| Setting | Value | Why |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Campaign name | `NordVPN - Search - Test 1` | Easy to identify later |
| Networks | **Uncheck** "Google Display Network" and **Uncheck** "Google Search Partners" | You only want Google Search — Display and Partners are low quality for this test |
| Target locations | **United States** | Highest NordVPN payouts and purchase intent |
| Language | **English** | Match your ad copy |
| Daily budget | **$10** | $30 total test over 3 days |

### 5.5 Bidding strategy

1. Select **"Maximize clicks"** (simplest for testing)
2. Set **Maximum CPC bid limit**: **$1.50**
3. This caps what you'll pay per click — prevents Google from overspending on expensive clicks

> **Why $1.50 cap**: VPN keywords can cost $2-5+ without a cap. At $1.50
> you'll get fewer clicks but stay within budget. If your ads aren't
> getting impressions after Day 1, raise this to $2.00.

## 6. Create Your First Ad

### 6.1 Set up an ad group

1. Ad group name: `VPN Deals - Phrase Match`
2. This is where you enter your keywords

### 6.2 Enter keywords

Copy-paste these keywords exactly (the quotes mean "phrase match" — your ad
shows when someone searches a phrase containing these words):

```
"best vpn deal"
"vpn discount"
"vpn discount code"
"cheap vpn"
"nordvpn coupon"
"nordvpn deal"
"nordvpn discount"
"vpn deal 2026"
"best vpn 2026"
"vpn sale"
```

> **Why phrase match**: Broad match wastes money on irrelevant searches.
> Exact match is too restrictive for a test. Phrase match is the sweet spot —
> your ad shows when the search contains your phrase, plus extra words.

### 6.3 Add negative keywords

Go to **"Negative keywords"** and add these (prevents your ad from showing
for useless searches):

```
free
crack
cracked
torrent
download
review
reddit
what is
how does
```

> **Why negative keywords matter**: Without these, you'll pay for clicks from
> people searching "free vpn" or "nordvpn review reddit" who will never buy.

### 6.4 Write the ad

Google uses **Responsive Search Ads**. You provide multiple headlines and
descriptions, and Google mixes/matches them.

**Headlines** (enter all of these — max 30 characters each):

| # | Headline |
|---|----------|
| 1 | Best VPN Deal 2026 - 72% Off |
| 2 | NordVPN Verified Discount |
| 3 | 30-Day Money Back Guarantee |
| 4 | #1 Rated VPN - Lowest Price |
| 5 | NordVPN Sale - Save 72% |
| 6 | Protect All Your Devices |
| 7 | NordVPN 72% Off Today Only |
| 8 | Fast, Secure & Affordable VPN |

**Descriptions** (enter all of these — max 90 characters each):

| # | Description |
|---|-------------|
| 1 | Get the #1 rated VPN at the lowest price available. Fast speeds on all devices. |
| 2 | No risk. Full refund within 30 days if you're not satisfied. Limited time offer. |
| 3 | One account protects 6 devices. Works with Netflix and 50+ streaming services. |
| 4 | Verified working deal for 2026. Military-grade encryption at 72% off. |

**Final URL**: Your Carrd landing page URL (e.g., `https://bestvpndeal2026.carrd.co`)

**Display path**: `best-vpn/deal` (this shows in the ad as `yoursite.carrd.co/best-vpn/deal` — cosmetic only, doesn't need to be a real page)

### 6.5 Pin your best headlines (optional but recommended)

1. Click the pin icon next to Headline 1: **"Best VPN Deal 2026 - 72% Off"**
2. Pin it to **Position 1** (ensures it always shows first)
3. This is your strongest headline — don't let Google rotate it out

### 6.6 Review and launch

1. Review everything on the summary screen
2. Double-check: daily budget = $10, location = US, Search only
3. Click **"Publish Campaign"**
4. Google will review the ad (usually approved within 1-4 hours)
5. If rejected: check the rejection reason email — usually it's a policy issue with the landing page. Fix and resubmit.

## 7. Launch & Wait 3 Days

### Day 1: Verify ads are running

Check these within 24 hours of launch:

- [ ] Log into Google Ads dashboard
- [ ] Confirm campaign status is **"Eligible"** or **"Active"**
- [ ] Check you have at least **some impressions** (even 10-20 is fine)
- [ ] If status is "Not eligible" or "Ad disapproved", read the reason and fix it

**Common Day 1 issues**:

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| Ad disapproved | Check email for reason. Usually: misleading claims or landing page issues. Remove "only" from "today only", make sure your page has a privacy policy link |
| Zero impressions | Your bids are too low. Raise Max CPC to $2.00 |
| Impressions but 0 clicks | Your ad copy isn't compelling. Rewrite Headline 1 |
| Spending too fast | Lower Max CPC to $1.00 |

### Day 2: First look at data

- Check your **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: the percentage of people who saw your ad and clicked
  - **Good**: 3%+ CTR
  - **OK**: 1-3% CTR
  - **Bad**: < 1% CTR (rewrite ad copy)
- Check your **average CPC**: what you're actually paying per click
- Check the **Search Terms report** (in Google Ads → Keywords → Search Terms): see the actual phrases people searched. Add any irrelevant ones as negative keywords.

### Day 3: Conversion check

- Log into your **NordVPN affiliate dashboard**
- Check if any sales came through
- Cross-reference with your Google Ads click data

> **Important**: There can be a 24-48 hour delay between when someone clicks
> and when the sale shows in your affiliate dashboard. Don't panic if Day 2
> shows zero — check again on Day 4.

## 8. Read the Numbers

After spending ~$30 (3 days at $10/day), calculate your results:

### The formula

```
Cost Per Click (CPC)   = Total Spend ÷ Total Clicks
Conversion Rate (CR)   = Total Sales ÷ Total Clicks × 100
Revenue Per Click (EPC) = Total Commission ÷ Total Clicks
Profit                 = Total Commission - Total Spend
ROI                    = (Profit ÷ Total Spend) × 100
```

### Example: Profitable scenario

```
Spent:        $30
Clicks:       30 (CPC = $1.00)
Sales:        1
Commission:   $100

Profit:       $100 - $30 = $70
ROI:          233%
EPC:          $3.33
```

### Example: Break-even scenario

```
Spent:        $30
Clicks:       25 (CPC = $1.20)
Sales:        0
Commission:   $0

Profit:       -$30
```

But check affiliate dashboard again in 48 hours — the cookie lasts 30 days, so
delayed conversions are common. Someone who clicked today might buy next week.

### Benchmark: What "good" looks like

| Metric | Bad | OK | Good |
|--------|-----|----|----- |
| CTR | < 1% | 1-3% | 3%+ |
| CPC | > $2.00 | $1.00-2.00 | < $1.00 |
| Conversion Rate | 0% | 1-2% | 3%+ |
| ROI | Negative | 0-50% | 50%+ |

## 9. Decide: Scale, Optimize, or Pivot

### Scenario A: You got 1+ sales (profitable)

**Action: Scale gradually**

1. Increase daily budget to **$20/day**
2. Let it run 5 more days
3. If still profitable, increase to **$30/day**
4. Add more keywords:
   ```
   "vpn for streaming"
   "best vpn for netflix"
   "online privacy tool"
   "vpn subscription deal"
   ```
5. Create a second ad group with slightly different copy to test
6. **Never** increase budget more than 30% at a time — sudden jumps reset Google's algorithm

### Scenario B: 30+ clicks but 0 sales

**Action: Optimize the landing page**

Your ads are working (people are clicking), but the page isn't converting.

Try these changes one at a time (wait 2-3 days between each):

1. **Change the headline** — try urgency: "72% Off Ends This Week"
2. **Add social proof** — "Trusted by 14 million users worldwide"
3. **Add a comparison table** — NordVPN vs ExpressVPN vs Surfshark (NordVPN wins on price)
4. **Move the CTA button higher** — some visitors don't scroll
5. **Add a second CTA button** at the bottom of the page

### Scenario C: Few clicks (< 15 in 3 days)

**Action: Fix the ads**

Your ads aren't compelling enough or your bids are too low.

1. Raise Max CPC to **$2.00**
2. Add more keywords (see list in Scenario A)
3. Rewrite headlines — focus on specific numbers: "72% Off", "$3.09/month"
4. Check Search Terms report — are you showing for the right searches?

### Scenario D: High CPC (> $2.50) eating your budget

**Action: Find cheaper keywords**

1. Pause the expensive keywords
2. Try longer, more specific phrases (called "long-tail keywords"):
   ```
   "cheapest vpn for streaming 2026"
   "nordvpn student discount"
   "vpn deal under 5 dollars"
   "best vpn monthly plan"
   ```
3. Long-tail keywords have less competition = lower CPC

## 11. Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Sending traffic directly to the affiliate link

Google will **ban your account**. Always use a landing page in between.

### Mistake 2: Using broad match keywords

Broad match means Google shows your ad for vaguely related searches. You'll pay
for clicks from people searching "what is a VPN" (information seekers, not buyers).
Always use **"phrase match"** (with quotes) or [exact match] (with brackets).

### Mistake 3: Not checking the Search Terms report

This report shows the **actual words people typed** before clicking your ad. Check
it on Day 2 and add irrelevant terms as negative keywords. This alone can cut
wasted spend by 30-50%.

### Mistake 4: Changing too many things at once

If you change the ad copy AND the landing page AND the keywords on the same day,
you won't know which change helped (or hurt). Change **one variable at a time**,
wait 2-3 days, then evaluate.

### Mistake 5: Giving up after 1 failed test

Most affiliate marketers test 3-5 offers before finding a winner. A $30 test that
fails is not a failure — it's $30 worth of data. The winner pays for all the tests.

### Mistake 6: Not setting a daily budget cap

Always set a daily budget. Without it, Google will spend as much as it can. $10/day
is your test budget — don't let it creep up until you have proven profitability.

### Mistake 7: Ignoring mobile

60%+ of Google searches are mobile. Make sure your landing page looks good on a
phone. Carrd handles this automatically, but always preview.

### Mistake 8: Making income claims in ads

Never say "Make money with VPN" or similar in your ads. Google will reject them.
Focus on the product's benefits to the end user.

## 12. FAQ

### Q: Is this legal?

Yes. Affiliate marketing is a legitimate business model used by millions of people
and major companies. You're essentially acting as a paid referral partner. Just make
sure your landing page has an affiliate disclosure (a small line at the bottom:
"This page contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.")

### Q: Do I need a business entity?

Not to start. Most affiliate networks accept individual sign-ups. Once you're making
consistent money ($1000+/month), consider setting up an LLC for tax and liability
purposes.

### Q: What about taxes?

Affiliate commissions are taxable income. The affiliate network will send you a
1099 form at year-end (in the US) if you earn over $600. Keep records of your ad
spend — it's tax-deductible as a business expense.

### Q: Can I do this outside the US?

Yes. You can run Google Ads targeting the US from anywhere. The affiliate commission
is paid regardless of your location. Payment methods vary by network (PayPal, wire
transfer, Payoneer are common).

### Q: What if Google disapproves my ad?

Read the disapproval reason in your email or Google Ads dashboard. Common fixes:
- Remove superlatives ("best", "fastest") unless you can cite a source
- Add an affiliate disclosure to your landing page
- Remove urgency language that might be seen as misleading ("today only")
- Make sure your landing page has a privacy policy link (Carrd has a template for this)

### Q: How long before I make real money?

Realistic timeline:
- **Week 1-2**: Testing, learning, possibly losing $30-60
- **Week 3-4**: Finding a winning combo or pivoting to new offers
- **Month 2**: If you found a winner, scaling to $10-50/day profit
- **Month 3-6**: Optimizing to $50-200/day profit
- This is not a get-rich-quick scheme. It's a learnable, scalable skill.

### Q: Can I use other ad platforms instead of Google?

Yes, but Google Search is best for beginners because:
- People are searching with **buying intent** ("vpn deal" = ready to buy)
- Facebook/TikTok require you to **create** demand with attention-grabbing creatives
- Google is "pull" marketing (people come to you), social is "push" (you interrupt them)
- Start with Google Search. Add Facebook/TikTok later once you know what converts.

### Q: What's the minimum budget to get meaningful data?

$30 is the minimum useful test. You need at least 20-30 clicks to judge whether a
landing page converts. At $1.00 CPC, that's $20-30. Anything less and you're just
guessing from noise.
