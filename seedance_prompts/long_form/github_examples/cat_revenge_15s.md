# 真實案例：白貓復仇短劇（15s，5 段分鏡）

來源：GitHub [gracech0322-cmd/seedance-2-prompt-library](https://github.com/gracech0322-cmd/seedance-2-prompt-library)

做法：完整敘事弧拆成 5 個時間塊，每塊含獨立的情緒節拍、場景切換、角色動作與配樂變化。可整段一次生成，或每塊獨立生成一個 clip 再拼。

```
0-4s: On a cold rainy night, a white cat sits by a trash can. Her fur is wet and she looks weak and heartbroken.
```
```
4-8s: The white cat suddenly stands up in the rain and her eyes change from weak to cold. A lightning flash cuts to a luxury dressing room.
```
```
8-11s: In a bright ballroom, the white cat enters with a black cat prince. She wears a purple lace dress and looks confident.
```
```
11-13s: The Siamese cat walks toward the white cat with a glass of wine and pretends to slip to spill it on her.
```
```
13-15s: The white cat calmly looks down, then turns away and walks out with the black cat prince.
```
