# Continuity Bible

## Must Stay Fixed

- Main cat wording must remain exactly the same as the locked character block in `visual_bible.md`.
- The cat must remain a realistic gray-white stray kitten, not a cartoon cat, not a large adult cat.
- The environment must remain the same storm road with broken fence, ruined house, tornado, dust haze, and dark cloud cover.
- Overall mood must stay tragic and helpless, not heroic or action-oriented.
- Camera language must stay close, intimate, and slightly handheld.

## Allowed Variation

- The cat may shift from stillness to one small struggle-forward step.
- Dust density, debris motion, and tornado rotation can vary slightly.
- Camera can move from static to a subtle push-in.

## Evolution Rules

- First beat: shocked stare into distance.
- Middle beat: stronger wind, more debris, emotional hesitation.
- Final beat: tiny forward attempt, then frozen grief in the storm.

## Drift Recovery Lines

- 保持同一只灰白相间瘦小流浪幼猫，脸型偏尖、耳朵略大、圆而湿润的眼睛、粉色鼻尖、真实动物比例、电影级写实毛发细节。
- 保持同一处荒凉郊外道路、破旧柏油路、倾斜围栏、破败低矮房屋、远处巨大龙卷风、黄灰色尘雾与乌云压顶的末日环境。
- 保持伤感无力的情绪表达，不要变成战斗、冒险或奇幻英雄风格。
